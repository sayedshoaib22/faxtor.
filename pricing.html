Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

What's included in the project price?

Each plan includes development, testing, deployment, and support as specified. Additional features or changes may incur extra costs, which we'll discuss upfront.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time. Downgrades are available at the end of your current project cycle.

Do you offer payment plans?

We offer flexible payment options. Typically, we require 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. For Enterprise plans, we can customize payment terms.

What happens after the support period ends?

After the included support period, you can opt for our maintenance plans or pay-as-you-go support. We'll ensure your software continues to run smoothly.
